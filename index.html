About

Welcome to my academic homepage. I am a researcher and educator with interests spanning multiple disciplines including computer science, data science, and interdisciplinary studies.

My work focuses on the intersection of theory and practice, with an emphasis on developing novel approaches to complex problems. I am committed to both advancing knowledge through research and fostering the next generation of scholars through teaching.
